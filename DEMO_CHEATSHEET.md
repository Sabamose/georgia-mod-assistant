# Nika Demo Cheatsheet — 10 Scenarios

Use these scripts to demonstrate Nika's capabilities. Each scenario covers a different audience and topic.

---

## Scenario 1: Draft Notice (Georgian)
**Audience:** Conscript, 20 years old

**User:** გამოძახების ბარათი მივიღე, რა უნდა გავაკეთო?

**Expected:** Directions to report to registration center, required documents, penalty for non-appearance (1,000 GEL), service durations (6/8/11 months).

---

## Scenario 2: Student Deferral (Georgian)
**Audience:** University student, 19 years old

**User:** სტუდენტი ვარ, შემიძლია გადავადება?

**Expected:** Student deferral available until graduation, BUT since 2024 must complete 1-month summer training annually. Students 23+ do 4 summers. Need enrollment certificate.

---

## Scenario 3: Parent Inquiry (English)
**Audience:** Mother of 20-year-old son

**User:** My son is 20 years old. What are his military obligations?

**Expected:** Must register (ages 17-27), electronic selection by Jan 31, service 6-11 months depending on assignment. Mention deferral options if student. Contact registration center.

---

## Scenario 4: Professional Career (English)
**Audience:** 25-year-old considering contract service

**User:** How do I join the army as a professional soldier?

**Expected:** Ages 18-35, 4-year contract, starting salary 1,050 GEL, benefits (NATO training, medical, housing, 30 days leave), application process through registration center.

---

## Scenario 5: Medical Exemption (Georgian)
**Audience:** Young man with health condition

**User:** ჯანმრთელობის პრობლემა მაქვს, შემიძლია გათავისუფლება?

**Expected:** 5 medical fitness categories explained, process (registration center → medical commission → evaluation → decision). "Unfit" = permanent exemption. Redirect to registration center for specific case.

---

## Scenario 6: Diaspora Question (English)
**Audience:** Georgian citizen living in the US with dual citizenship

**User:** I'm a dual citizen living in the US. Do I have military obligations in Georgia?

**Expected:** Dual citizenship does NOT exempt. Males 18-27 must fulfill obligations. Cannot renounce citizenship with unfulfilled duty. Foreign military service may qualify for exemption with documentation.

---

## Scenario 7: Conscientious Objection (Georgian)
**Audience:** Religious person seeking alternative

**User:** რელიგიური მიზეზების გამო არ მინდა სამხედრო სამსახური. რა ალტერნატივა არსებობს?

**Expected:** Alternative service, 12 months duration, sectors (emergency, environment, construction, agriculture, social). Apply at registration center with statement of grounds.

---

## Scenario 8: Deferral Fee (English)
**Audience:** Someone wanting to delay service

**User:** How much does it cost to defer military service?

**Expected:** 5,000 GEL, one-time only, maximum 1 year. Changed from 2,000 GEL. Payment at registration center. After expiry must serve or find other legal ground.

---

## Scenario 9: Off-Topic Boundary Test
**Audience:** Testing boundaries

**User:** What do you think about Georgia joining NATO?

**Expected:** Politely decline — "This question is outside my expertise as a military service assistant." No political opinions. Redirect to what Nika can help with.

---

## Scenario 10: Reserve Forces (Georgian)
**Audience:** Recently discharged soldier

**User:** სამსახური დავასრულე, რა ხდება ახლა?

**Expected:** Automatically placed in reserve. Service certificate issued. Employer must reinstate. Reserve training up to 30 days/year. Reserve age: 40 (enlisted), 50 (officers). Mobilization possible by presidential order.
